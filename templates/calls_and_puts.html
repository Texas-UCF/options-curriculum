{% extends "base.html" %} {% block title %} Calls and Puts {% endblock title %} {% block content %} There are two types of options1:
  • Calls give the owner the right to buy something
  • Puts give the owner the right to sell something
It's important to recognize that buying calls and buying puts do not give opposite outcomes. Instead, buying calls and selling calls give opposite outcomes, and the same goes for buying and selling puts. Thus, if I buy a call from you, we will have opposite outcomes; my profit will be your loss (ignoring any transaction fees). {% endblock content %} {% block parentheticals %} 1. Calls and puts may give owners completely different rights, but eventually you'll learn that the difference is almost negligible. {% endblock parentheticals %}
